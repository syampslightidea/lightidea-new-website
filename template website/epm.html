Comprehensive Planning and Budgeting

LightIdea leverages Oracle Planning and Budgeting Cloud Service (PBCS) to streamline your planning processes. Our experts help you:

  • Simplify financial planning and operational budgeting.
  • Implement driver-based models for better accuracy.
  • Enable rolling forecasts to adapt to changing conditions.

Efficient Financial Close and Consolidation

We optimize your financial close process with Oracle Financial Consolidation and Close (FCCS). LightIdea ensures:

  • Faster and accurate consolidation.
  • Automated intercompany eliminations.
  • Compliance with accounting standards like GAAP and IFRS.

Profitability and Cost Management

Gain deeper insights into cost drivers and profitability with Oracle Profitability and Cost Management Cloud Service (PCMCS). Our solutions enable:

  • Allocation-based costing.
  • Advanced profitability analysis.
  • Scenario modeling for informed decision-making.

Strategic Modeling and Reporting

LightIdea empowers organizations with Oracle Narrative Reporting and Strategic Modeling tools to:

  • Perform long-term financial planning.
  • Collaborate on comprehensive narrative reports.
  • Conduct what-if scenarios to evaluate business impacts.
